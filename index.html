Tips and Tricks: Meditation for Beginners

BY WINSTON FITZGERALD KING

Paws and reflect. These days, humans are constantly working with little time to play. This leads to an increase in stress, anxiety, and depression. While there are many remedies out there for these issues, my recommendation is to try meditation. The best part? You can do this with your furry friend! Below this article, I included a couple of videos that guide you through meditating with your fur baby.
