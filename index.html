Tips and Tricks: Meditation for Beginners

BY WINSTON FITZGERALD KING

Paws and reflect. These days, humans are constantly working with little time to play. This leads to an increase in stress, anxiety, and depression. While there are many remedies out there for these issues, my recommendation is to try meditation. The best part? You can do this with your furry friend! Meditation has many health benefits and provides a calm, clear mind. I recommend sitting in a quiet space with your fur baby and take a moment to focus on your breathing. There are many different ways to meditate so be sure to search for the method that works best for you.
